The Problem

Your School Is Paying to Use Software
It Will Never Own.

Most private schools in Nigeria have quietly accumulated a portfolio of tools they depend on entirely yet own nothing. Each academic year, the invoices return. Each renewal cycle, your institution's continuity is held against a payment deadline.

01

The Annual Cost Trap

Schools spend between ₦300,000 and ₦800,000 every year on subscriptions — tools that generate zero balance sheet value. Over five years, that is a minimum of ₦1.5 million with no institutional asset to show for it.

02

Total Vendor Dependency

When a vendor change pricing, your school absorbs the consequences. Your academic calendar and staff operations should not be subject to decisions made by external companies you cannot control.

03

No True Data Ownership

Where does your student data reside? What happens to five years of academic records if a vendor shuts down or doubles pricing? Schools that rent their software do not control their own institutional data.

₦4M+

A school spending ₦800,000 per year on subscriptions will have spent over ₦4 million within five academic years across tools they do not own, cannot customise, and may lose access to without warning.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the Questions
Decision-Makers Ask First

Is this a subscription model? Will we be billed annually?

No. School Ease operates on a one-time project investment model. You pay for the design, build, and deployment of your system. There are no annual licence fees, no renewal invoices, and no ongoing subscription obligations — to School Ease or to any third-party platform associated with the deployment.

What happens after the system goes live?

Every deployment includes a defined post-launch support period during which our team remains available to address issues and ensure the system is performing as specified. Following this period, your institution operates the system independently. Extended support arrangements can be discussed separately if required.

What does ongoing technical support look like?

Post-deployment support is included during the handover window in every package. For ongoing maintenance, system updates, or future module additions, we offer clearly defined engagement terms scoped and agreed upon separately — never bundled into a mandatory recurring fee.

Can we select specific modules rather than a full package?

Yes. During your consultation, we will scope a deployment plan that reflects your school's specific priorities. You are not required to take modules your institution does not yet need futhermore, deferred modules can be added in future phases without rebuilding existing infrastructure.

Is School Ease suitable for our school's size?

School Ease is purpose-built for mid-sized private primary and secondary schools in Nigeria with 300 to 1,200+ students. Our systems are configured specifically for the operational structures common to this segment — multi-arm classes, term-based calendars, and Nigerian private school administrative workflows.
